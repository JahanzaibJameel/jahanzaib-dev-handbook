<h1 id="environment-management"><a class="header" href="#environment-management">Environment Management</a></h1> <p>Environment management is crucial for maintaining consistency across development, staging, and production environments while keeping sensitive data secure.</p> <h2 id="-what-is-environment-management"><a class="header" href="#-what-is-environment-management">🎯 What is Environment Management?</a></h2> <p>Environment management involves configuring different settings for various deployment environments (development, staging, production) while ensuring security and consistency.</p> <h3 id="why-environment-management-matters"><a class="header" href="#why-environment-management-matters">Why Environment Management Matters</a></h3> <ul> <li><strong>Security</strong>: Keep sensitive data out of version control</li> <li><strong>Consistency</strong>: Ensure consistent behavior across environments</li> <li><strong>Flexibility</strong>: Easy switching between environments</li> <li><strong>Debugging</strong>: Environment-specific logging and features</li> <li><strong>Deployment</strong>: Smooth transitions between environments</li> </ul> <h2 id="-environment-variables"><a class="header" href="#-environment-variables">🔧 Environment Variables</a></h2> <h3 id="environment-variable-types"><a class="header"
