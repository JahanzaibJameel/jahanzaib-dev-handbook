Community Guidelines

Be Respectful and Inclusive

  • Welcome developers of all skill levels
  • Respect different perspectives and backgrounds
  • Use inclusive language and be patient with beginners
  • No discrimination or harassment of any kind

Help Others Learn

  • Share your knowledge and experience
  • Provide constructive feedback
  • Answer questions when you can
  • Celebrate others’ successes

Stay On Topic

  • Keep discussions relevant to development
  • Use appropriate channels for different topics
  • Avoid spam and self-promotion
  • Follow the channel-specific guidelines

Code of Conduct

All community members must follow our Code of Conduct. Violations will result in warnings and potential removal from the community.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the community really free?

A: Yes! All basic community features are free. We offer premium mentorship and advanced courses for those who want extra support.

Q: I’m a complete beginner. Is this community for me?

A: Absolutely! We have dedicated beginner channels and patient mentors who love helping newcomers.

Q: How can I become a mentor?

A: Once you’ve been active in the community and helped others, you can apply to become an official mentor through our mentorship program.

Q: Can I promote my own products/services?

A: Limited self-promotion is allowed in designated channels. Please read our community guidelines for specific rules.

Q: How do I report inappropriate behavior?

A: Contact our moderation team at report@jahanzaib.dev or use the report features in Discord and GitHub.
